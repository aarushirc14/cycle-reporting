June cadence

The user should always know where they are in the cycle: the forecast is monthly, the recommendations are weekly, and the data layer supports the evidence.

Leadership view

VPs and DPs should live mostly in the email, Performance Outlook, and weekly brief. They need the answer, the change, the action, and optional proof.

Doer view

Sales and marketing teams use the weekly brief as the packaged internal readout, then open Community Pulse only when they need to inspect the underlying evidence.

Email notification

The email mirrors the original prototype structure, but now makes two things explicit: what to act on now and what changed since the last update. Every claim in the email appears again in the weekly landing page.

1. Critical priorityRecommended action · Audit Website

Esprit at Avenir

Top-of-funnel is healthy — 23,822 active users vs ~13,000 needed. The page only converts 0.46% to leads vs the 1.5% benchmark, and first-visit-to-sale is weak too.

-7sales at risk
Costing roughly 7 sales because Web to Lead at 0.46% vs 1.5% benchmark leaks qualified shoppers before they raise a hand.
Do next

Run a website and offer review on the Esprit page. Add buyer-fit statement and Esprit vs L'Ambiance comparison. Bring HOA, 55+, and Avenir amenity details forward.

Don't yet

Don't add broad traffic — the page already has volume it isn't converting. Don't reuse L'Ambiance's offer wholesale.

This matches the Performance Outlook diagnostic signal: demand exists, but buyer-fit and offer clarity may be suppressing conversion.

2. Critical priorityRecommended action · Audit Website

Verano

Traffic gap is small — only ~800 users short. The page tries to be everything to everyone, and the next step isn't obvious enough.

-4sales at risk
Costing roughly 4 sales because Web to Lead and First Visit to Sale both run below benchmark on near-target traffic.
Do next

Split the page into clear buyer paths: golf, pickleball/social, quick-move-in, value, and PGA adjacency. Replace generic tour CTA with path-specific next steps.

Don't yet

Don't broaden top-of-funnel spend. Don't make a price-position move until buyer-fit clarity work lands.

The recommendation is not “web traffic is low.” The diagnostic signal is that the existing page does not separate buyer motivations clearly enough.
